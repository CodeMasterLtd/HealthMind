Private Policy

Effective Date: 14/07/2024

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your username, password, and email address.
  • Health Information: If you choose to provide it, we may collect information such as height, weight, age, and other health-related data to provide personalized health tips and services.
  • Notes: Any notes or comments you choose to enter in your profile.

Usage Data

  • Analytics: We may collect usage data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited to improve our services and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To personalize your experience and provide tailored health tips and recommendations.
  • To communicate with you, including providing customer support and sending service-related announcements.
  • To improve our services and develop new features.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing our services (e.g., hosting providers, analytics providers).
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and use. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your account information at any time by logging into your account settings.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the instructions provided in the email.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with a new effective date.
